Archetypes are pre-made character builds with a particular focus. Use them directly, or as inspiration to generate your own charater. Each has a \emph{primary} and a \emph{secondary} stat. Use \textbf{+6} as the primary stat, \textbf{0} as the secondary stat, and \textbf{-5} as the remaining stat.

\begin{multicols}{2}

\subsection{Brawler}
\prop{Primary stat}{Brawn}\\
\prop{Secondary stat}{Wit}\\
\prop{Max HP}{36}\\
\prop{Equipment}{knuckle dusters, polycarb spaulders, combat knife, history relic}

\subsection{Strategist}
\prop{Primary stat}{Brawn}\\
\prop{Secondary stat}{Brains}\\
\prop{Max HP}{36}\\
\prop{Equipment}{Remington 870 \& 4 shells, leathers}

\end{multicols}

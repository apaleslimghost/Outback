Archetypes are pre-made character builds with a particular focus. Use them directly, or as inspiration to generate your own charater. Each has a \emph{primary} and a \emph{secondary} stat. Use \textbf{17} as the primary stat, \textbf{14} as the secondary stat, and \textbf{9} as the remaining stat.

\begin{multicols}{2}

\subsection{Brawler}
\prop{Primary stat}{Brawn}\\
\prop{Secondary stat}{Wit}\\
\prop{Skills}{agility, unarmed, melee weapons}\\
\prop{Equipment}{knuckle dusters, polycarb spaulders, combat knife, history relic}

\subsection{Strategist}
\prop{Primary stat}{Brawn}\\
\prop{Secondary stat}{Brains}\\
\prop{Skills}{armour, athletics, lore}\\
\prop{Equipment}{Remington 870 sawn-off \& 4 rounds, leathers}

\end{multicols}
